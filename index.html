About This Visualization

This dumbbell chart displays the average playing time patterns for Liverpool FC players during the 2015/16 Premier League season. Each row represents a player, with the blue dot showing their average entry minute and the red dot showing their average exit minute. The connecting line represents their typical time on the pitch.

Interactions: Hover over any element to see detailed statistics. Use the controls above to sort and filter the data. The chart animates when data changes.
